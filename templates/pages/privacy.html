What information is collected

The appointment form may collect patient name, phone number, preferred department, preferred doctor, preferred date/time, and a short message or reason for visit.

How it is used

Submitted details are used by hospital staff only for hospital communication, appointment follow-up, and patient service coordination.

What not to submit

Do not submit Aadhaar numbers, payment details, prescriptions, lab reports, detailed medical records, or sensitive documents through the public website.

Access control

Appointment requests are not publicly visible. Admin and dashboard access are protected by staff login.
