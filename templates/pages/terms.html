Website purpose

This website provides hospital information, public contact details, OPD timing information, and appointment request support.

Appointment requests

Submitting an appointment form does not guarantee a confirmed appointment. Hospital staff must contact the patient to confirm timing and availability.

Emergency cases

For urgent medical concerns, call the hospital directly or visit the nearest appropriate emergency facility. Do not wait for online form confirmation.

Information accuracy

Phone numbers, timings, services, and facility details should be confirmed by the hospital before launch and kept updated through the admin panel.
